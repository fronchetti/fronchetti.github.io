Thanks to the great advisors I had in my life, I was able to transform my dreams into reality. Now, it is my turn to support students.

Prospective students

If you are interested in joining my team, feel free to contact me by email. Introduce yourself in your message, explain your intentions, and make sure to attach your curriculum vitae. At the present moment, I am hiring in all categories, from undergraduate to graduate students.

Admission of graduate students

Students interested in applying for graduate positions under my mentorship must first comprehend the expectations and admission requirements of our department. Graduate applicants from other countries are also welcomed in my team. I may have funding to support graduate students throughout their journey at Louisiana State. If you are interested in having me as your advisor, please contact me before applying.
