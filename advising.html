Thanks to the great advisors I had in my life, I was able to transform my dreams into reality. Now, it is my turn to support new students.

Prospective students

If you are interested in joining my team as a student, feel free to contact me at ffronchetti@lsu.edu. Introduce yourself in your message, and make sure to attach your curriculum vitae. At the present moment, I am hiring in all categories, from undergraduate to graduate students. If I don't respond to your message within a week, send me a reminder, sometimes things get busy on my end. Students interested in a Ph.D. or M.Sc. position must first comprehend the expectations of our department before applying.

Admission of international students

Graduate applicants from other countries are also welcomed in my team. For graduate positions, I may have funding to support the financial costs of students. If you are interested, make sure you comply with our admission requirements before applying. I am open to answer any questions, feel free to reach me out!
