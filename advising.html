Prospective students

If you are interested in joining my team, feel free to contact me by email. Introduce yourself in your message, explain your intentions, and make sure to attach your curriculum vitae. At the present moment, I am hiring in all categories, from undergraduate to graduate students.

Admission of graduate students

Students interested in graduate positions must first comprehend the expectations and admission requirements of our department before applying. Graduate applicants from other countries are also welcomed in my team. For graduate positions, I may have funding to financially support your life in the US. If you are interested, make sure you comply with our admission requirements and contact me before applying.
